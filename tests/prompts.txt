# --- Longer contextual prompts ---
Someone just got back from a trip and is telling the group about it. Have a conversation where they share highlights and others ask questions.

# --- Edge cases likely to trigger quote issues ---
Have a debate where people frequently quote each other's earlier points.
Someone is passive-aggressive and keeps referencing things said earlier.
A conversation where someone keeps bringing up what someone else said.
Have a heated argument where people quote each other to call out inconsistencies.
A conversation where someone agrees with an earlier point by quoting it back.
Have a discussion where someone extensively quotes a previous statement to dissect it.
A conversation where quotes are used for sarcasm or mockery.
Discuss a text conversation someone shared with the group, quoting parts of it.
Someone reads out loud from an article and others react to specific quotes.
Have a conversation where someone's earlier words come back to haunt them.
